What's Included in an Electrical Safety Audit

Comprehensive inspection, clear report

Our safety audits cover every aspect of your electrical system. We test, inspect and document the condition of your wiring, switchboard, safety switches, smoke alarms, and all connected equipment.

You get a detailed report with any faults found, risk ratings, and recommended fixes — prioritised by urgency.

  • Switchboard condition & labelling
  • Safety switch (RCD) trip-time testing
  • Circuit breaker load testing
  • Earth fault loop impedance testing
  • Smoke alarm compliance check
  • Visual inspection of wiring & connections
  • Power point & light switch condition
  • Written report with findings & recommendations

Who Needs One

Who Should Get an Electrical Safety Audit?

Landlords & Property Managers

Victorian rental regulations require electrical safety. Protect your tenants and meet your legal obligations with a professional compliance check.

Homeowners

Older homes may have outdated wiring, missing safety switches, or deteriorated connections. An audit identifies hidden hazards before they cause harm.

Businesses & Workplaces

OHS legislation requires employers to maintain safe electrical systems. Regular testing and tagging keeps your workplace compliant and your staff safe.

Before Buying a Property

A pre-purchase electrical inspection reveals costly issues that a building inspector might miss — old switchboards, aluminium wiring, or overloaded circuits.

Before Renovating

Know the condition of your electrical system before starting a renovation. Avoid surprises mid-build by identifying upgrades needed upfront.

After Storm or Flood Damage

Water and electrical damage go hand-in-hand. We inspect for moisture ingress, earth faults, and damaged wiring after weather events.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled Preventive Maintenance

Stop problems before they start

Regular scheduled maintenance catches deteriorating connections, overloaded circuits, and worn components. It's the most cost-effective way to avoid emergency callouts and unplanned downtime.

We offer annual and bi-annual maintenance plans for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

  • Thermal imaging for hot spots
  • Connection torque testing
  • Load balancing across circuits
  • Battery backup & UPS testing
  • Emergency lighting checks
  • Detailed maintenance log
