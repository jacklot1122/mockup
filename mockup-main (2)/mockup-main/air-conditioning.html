Split Systems

Split System Air Conditioning Installation

Room-by-room comfort, energy efficient

Split system air conditioners are the most popular choice for cooling and heating individual rooms. We supply and install all major brands including Daikin, Mitsubishi, Fujitsu, Samsung and LG.

Every install includes proper sizing, electrical connection, refrigerant piping and a full commissioning test. We also handle the VEU rebate paperwork if your system qualifies.

  • Supply & install — all major brands
  • Proper sizing for your room & climate
  • Wall-mounted, floor-mounted & multi-split
  • Reverse cycle (heating & cooling)
  • VEU rebate-eligible models available
  • 5-year warranty on workmanship

Ducted Systems

Ducted Air Conditioning — Whole-Home Comfort

Discreet, powerful climate control for the whole house

Ducted air conditioning sits hidden in the ceiling and delivers heating and cooling to every room through discreet vents. Ideal for new builds, renovations, or replacing old gas ducted heating.

We design the duct layout, install the system, and make sure every zone is balanced for even temperature distribution.

  • Full ducted system design & install
  • Zone control for individual rooms
  • Gas ducted heater replacement
  • VEU rebates up to $5,530
  • Reverse cycle — heat & cool from one unit
  • Quiet operation, hidden from view

Servicing & Repairs

Air Conditioning Servicing & Repair

Keep your AC running efficiently year-round

Regular servicing extends the life of your air conditioner, improves energy efficiency, and catches small problems before they become expensive ones. We service all brands of split and ducted systems.

If your unit isn't cooling or heating properly, making strange noises, or leaking water — call us for a diagnosis.

  • Annual service & filter clean
  • Refrigerant leak detection & recharge
  • Compressor & fan motor repair
  • Thermostat & control issues
  • Drainage & condensation problems
  • All brands — residential & commercial
