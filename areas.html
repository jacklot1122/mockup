Sectors

We work with

Residential

Houses, townhouses, apartments, granny flats — from single powerpoint repairs to whole-home rewires, switchboard upgrades, and full AC installs.

Rental Properties

Compliance inspections, safety switch testing, smoke alarm checks, and air conditioning maintenance for landlords and property managers.

Commercial

Retail shops, offices, restaurants, and professional suites. Lighting fit-outs, power upgrades, and commercial air conditioning systems.

Industrial

Warehouses, factories, and workshops. Three-phase power, machine wiring, switchboard upgrades, and industrial cooling solutions.

Real Estate

Pre-sale electrical checks, tenant changeover inspections, and fast-turnaround maintenance for property managers and agencies.

Strata & Body Corporate

Common area lighting, switchboard maintenance, emergency exit lighting, and air conditioning for multi-unit developments.
