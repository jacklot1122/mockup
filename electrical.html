Switchboard Upgrades

Modern Switchboard Upgrades & Safety Switches

Upgrade your switchboard for safety & compliance

Old ceramic fuse boards are a fire risk and don't meet current Australian safety standards. We upgrade them to modern circuit breaker boards with safety switches (RCDs) to protect your family and property.

Required for rental properties, renovations, and any home wanting to add solar, EV chargers, or air conditioning.

  • Ceramic fuse board replacement
  • Safety switch (RCD) installation
  • Circuit breaker upgrades
  • Meter box relocations
  • Three-phase upgrades
  • Solar & EV charger readiness
