About FEAG

Licensed, Navy-trained, and local. Meet the bloke behind Frankston Electrical & Air Conditioning Group.

Ben Settree — Director, Electrician & HVAC Tech

Ben's a fully licensed sparky and air con tech with 20+ years under his belt across Melbourne and Sydney. He learned his trade in the Royal Australian Navy — and the discipline stuck.

He lives on the Peninsula with his wife and three kids, and proudly represents his Aboriginal heritage on Boonwurrung Country. Whether it's wiring, cooling, heating or an emergency at 2am — same honest service every time.

"Keep your home powered, comfortable, and safe — without the headache."

Background

How Ben got here

Royal Australian Navy

Ben started his electrical career in the Navy, where he trained as a marine electrical technician. The Navy gave him discipline, attention to detail, and the ability to work under pressure — skills that carry into every job.

20+ Years in the Trade

After the Navy, Ben spent two decades honing his craft across residential, commercial, and industrial sites in Melbourne and Sydney. From high-rise fit-outs to suburban switchboard upgrades — he's done it all.

HVAC Specialist

Ben holds refrigeration and air conditioning licences alongside his electrical contractor registration. That means one tradie for both your wiring and your cooling — fewer callouts, less cost.

Aboriginal Heritage

A proud Aboriginal man, Ben lives and works on Boonwurrung Country. FEAG acknowledges and respects the Traditional Owners of the land on which we operate, and Elders past, present and emerging.

Local & Family-Owned

Ben lives in Frankston with his wife and three kids. When you call FEAG, you're talking directly to the bloke who'll do the work — no call centres, no subcontractors rotating through.
